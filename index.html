Abby Coleman
BFA New Media
Biography
Abby Coleman is a 4th year student at the University of Lethbridge in Alberta, Canada, majoring in a BFA New Media with a minor in General Management. Abby has an interest in continuously learning about computer programs and technologies that forward Web design aesthetics and functions. Abby has studied a varied roster of classes within this degree, which has made her capable and amenable to many different tools to create design. A few of the projects Abby has collaborated on have appeared in the works of students on the University of Lethbridge website.
Artist Statement
I grew up in a household that encourages art in the workplace, the integration of beautiful design into everyday life. I chose to attend the University of Lethbridgeâ€™s New Media degree because it has a system of training artists to be adaptable to different mediums. My intention when entering this degree was to go into video game production but fell in love with the communicative and expressive design of the Web. I want to learn more about web coding languages to enhance my understanding of art and design, something that will be advancing as fast as the internet advances. I want to use my extensive skill set that I acquired from this degree to become important in a workplace I earn a career in. I am inspired by artists that are constantly learning the rules of design and how to break them in order to display their vision in a more creative manner.
